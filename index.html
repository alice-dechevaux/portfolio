About Me

I'm a Chicago-based software engineer with a passion for solving problems, building community, and fostering creativity. I believe in the power of technology to bring people together and I am always happy to be a part of projects that work toward that end. I design sites that are beautiful, functional, and fully responsive across all devices. You can find some of my previous projects here.

What I Offer

I build everything from static websites to dynamic, full-stack web applications. I'll work with you to custom design a site that fulfills all the needs of your business utilizing the following four step process:

Consultation:

It begins with a conversation through which I'll work to understand your business and how its needs will be best served. This is where we get to the heart of what your project is all about.

Proposal:

I'll present you with a detailed project description and visual preview to ensure that we're on the same page before I start building.

Creation:

This is where your project comes to life. I build and test your site while providing you with detailed and consistent progress updates along the way.

Launch & Support:

We introduce the world to your new website. Should you wish you may also choose to take advantage of ongoing maintenance and support options.
